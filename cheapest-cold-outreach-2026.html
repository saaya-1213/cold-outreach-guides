The Cheapest Way to Do Cold Outreach in 2026

A full outbound stack can run $100+/month. Here's how to do it for almost nothing and still get replies.

The cold email industry wants you to believe you need a $50–100/month platform to start. You don't. For your first hundred prospects, here's the lean setup that works.

The lean stack

  1. A sending inbox — a cheap separate domain + a mailbox (~$6–10/mo total). Set SPF/DKIM/DMARC.
  2. A lead list — built by hand or with a free finder. Quality over quantity: 30 good leads beat 3,000 random ones.
  3. A way to personalize & write — this is where most time goes, and where a small tool pays for itself.
  4. A tracker — to know your reply rate without a CRM.

What you can skip (for now)

  • A CRM — not until you have steady deal flow (here's why).
  • Expensive "warm-up networks" — just ramp volume slowly yourself.
  • Mass-sending platforms — they encourage volume, which is exactly what gets you blocked in 2026.

The math

Send 25 personalized emails/day for a month (~500 sends). At a realistic 8% reply rate, that's ~40 conversations — from a stack costing under $20/month plus a one-time tool. Compare that to $100+/month for software you're not using to capacity.
