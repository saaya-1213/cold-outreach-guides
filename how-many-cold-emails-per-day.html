How Many Cold Emails Should You Send Per Day?

Send too many too fast and you land in spam. Here are safe limits for 2026.

There's no single magic number — it depends on your domain's age and reputation. But the mistake everyone makes is the same: ramping volume too quickly. Here's a safe schedule.

Safe daily volume by domain age

Domain ageEmails/day per mailbox
Week 1 (new)≤20
Weeks 2–420–40, ramping gradually
1–3 months40–80
Established & warmed~100 (hard ceiling for cold)

Need more volume? Add more mailboxes (and ideally more domains) rather than pushing one inbox past ~100/day.

Why slow wins

Inbox providers watch for sudden spikes, low engagement, and spam complaints. A new domain blasting 500/day looks exactly like spam and gets throttled or blocked. Ramping slowly builds a sending reputation that keeps you in the inbox — see the full deliverability checklist.
