5 Cold Email Templates That Actually Get Replies

Plug-and-play B2B first-touch templates. Swap { } for real details. Updated 2026.

A template gets you 80% of the way; the personalization line does the rest. Keep every email to 50–70 words, make one point, and end with a soft ask. Here are five that work across common B2B niches.

1. SaaS / Startup

Subject: quick idea for {company}

Hi {name},
Saw your {recent funding/launch} — congrats. I noticed {specific detail}, and it reminded me of something teams at your stage often hit: outreach that stays personal as you scale.
I put together a no-monthly-tool way to do it. Worth a 10-min look?
Best, {you}

2. Ecommerce / DTC

Subject: re: your {product}

Hi {name},
Just came across {company} — {specific compliment}. Quick thought: your post-purchase emails could double as a B2B/wholesale channel.
I've got a simple way to run that outreach without another subscription. Open to a quick look?
{you}

3. Agency

Subject: helping {company}'s reply rate

Hi {name},
You're already running outreach for clients — so you know the bottleneck is personalization at volume.
I built a one-file tool that drafts a tailored first email per lead and tracks replies. Might save your team hours. Worth 10 minutes?
{you}

4. Consultant / Coach

Subject: {name}, quick question

Hi {name},
Booking calls usually comes down to consistent, personal outreach — which is the part that eats time.
I made a tiny tool that writes and tracks it. Happy to show you if useful.
{you}

5. The follow-up (sends after no reply)

Subject: last note, promise

Hi {name},
No worries if now's not the time. One angle I didn't mention: {new benefit}.
If it's worth a look, I'll send it over.
{you}
